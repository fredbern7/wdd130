Pohnpei

Pohnpei is the a tiny island and one of the four tiny island that made up a country. Pohnpei is known for its ancient ruins of Nan Madol. Pohnpei is also known for its unique cultural practices, including the ceremonial preparation of the Kava-like drink called sakau, and for its lush, adventurous landscape
